Style Prompt Pro Tips

3-5 tags max

Suno and Udio work best with 3 to 5 complementary tags. Overloading confuses the AI and produces inconsistent results.

Genre first always

Lead with your main genre. The first tag carries the most weight in directing the AI's musical style generation.

No negation

Describe what you want, not what you don't want. AI models interpret negative prompts poorly and often ignore them.

Production tags matter

Adding Studio Mastered, Reverb, or Stereo Wide can dramatically improve output quality and polish.
